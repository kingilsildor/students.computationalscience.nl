Jared Frazier

My experience in the master Computational Science (CLS) program jointly administered by the UvA and the VU was very positive! The course content prepared me for a broad array of computational work ranging from software engineering (scientific or otherwise) to more academic pursuits like a Ph.D. While my focus was in high performance computing, the master CLS program introduced me to topics in numerical partial differential equations, uncertainty quantification, and various performance optimization techniques that directly impact my work now as a research software engineer in weather and climate modelling. While I had no prior experience in the atmospheric physics associated with my current job, the flexibility of the master CLS program combined with the high quality theoretical and practical instruction greatly facilitated fast acquisition of the necessary skills to contribute to a domain outside my direct training. Lastly, the program coordinators were open to feedback from students and made curriculum changes based on student input, so this demonstrates the high degree of agency masters CLS students have in their own studies.

Souvik Chakraborty

My experience at the UvA was one of the most enriching experiences of my life. Right from the first day to the last I was pushed to be creative, to be curious, to be inquisitive. The atmosphere of collaborative development and the constant hunger for learning really shaped my outlook quite comprehensively. By meeting up with people from all over the world and with various different backgrounds, it was exciting to see how different minds think about a particular problem. It opened up possibilities that one wouldn’t think of and hence gave the opportunity to constantly be a sponge and keep on learning, how to adapt, change course if necessary and tackle the same problems. Personally, it was quite a journey for me as, coming from India, the environment is quite different here. The welcoming nature of every single individual that I came across gave me immense strength during this journey and for that I’m grateful to the UvA for giving me this opportunity that helped me evolve both personally and professionally. This master acted as a springboard for me from which I can now take on these learnings and apply them to the field I’m working on.
